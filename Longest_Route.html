    Longest Route

    The list of longest train route services of Indian Railways (which serves more than 2000 kms) contains regular passenger rail services that are currently scheduled and running directly between two cities of India which are more than 2,000 km (1,200 mi) apart. The special trains, coach changes, shunting or station transfers are not listed here.
